In the Beginning was the Isolation

Apr 14, 2020

A long time ago, I thought this could be a good idea…but as always there is no time. No time. Then this happened. Experts say this is the largest pandemic since 1918. Countries around the globe are under lockdown for a few months. Everyone is in isolation and now you have to fill all that time. Then this site comes to life…

Time to time I find out some interesting concepts that amaze me with their simplicity, but rarely I write about them..usually, I let them fly back from where they come. Here I will try to archive that. Besides archiving some interesting concepts and ideas that I found, I will write about my free time projects and other things that interest me.

Till the next post! #stayhome
